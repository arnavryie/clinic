Meet your physiotherapist

Dr. Giridhar Tagade

B.P.H.T. (Pune) · M.IAP · Senior Physiotherapist

Dr. Giridhar Tagade is a senior physiotherapist who has been practising for over 17 years. He is known across Mul and the wider Chandrapur region for the rehabilitation of differently-abled children and for bringing physiotherapy directly into people's homes through his home-care service.

His work spans neurological rehabilitation — including recovery after stroke and spinal cord injuries — as well as post-operative orthopaedic care such as knee replacement surgery and ligament injuries. Every treatment plan is built one-on-one, around the patient in front of him.

  • Bachelor of Physiotherapy — CMF's College of Physiotherapy, University of Pune
  • Life Member, Indian Association of Physiotherapists (IAP)
  • Registered with the Maharashtra Physiotherapy Council

PEMF therapy — gentle on you, tough on pain

Pulsed Electromagnetic Field (PEMF) therapy uses gentle electromagnetic pulses to support the body's own healing. It's non-invasive, drug-free and painless — often used alongside hands-on physiotherapy to help calm stubborn pain and aid tissue recovery.

  • No needles, no medication, no surgery
  • Comfortable enough for older patients
  • Pairs well with joint & spine rehabilitation

Good to know

Questions, answered

A few of the things people ask before their first visit.

Do you make home visits?
Yes. Dr. Tagade provides home-care physiotherapy across Mul and nearby areas in Chandrapur district — ideal for patients recovering from stroke, surgery or spinal injuries who find it hard to travel. Call us to arrange a visit.
Do I need a doctor's referral?
No referral is needed — you can come to us directly. If you do have a prescription, X-rays, MRI scans or reports, please bring them along so we can plan your treatment better.
What should I bring to my first visit?
Please bring any previous prescriptions, scans or medical reports related to your problem. That's all — we'll take care of the rest.
Do you treat children?
Absolutely. Rehabilitation for differently-abled children is one of Dr. Tagade's specialities, with years of experience and the patience these little patients deserve.
How long does a session take?
Most sessions last around 30–45 minutes, depending on your condition and treatment. Dr. Tagade will tell you what to expect and how many sessions are likely to help.
